Registration

Registration for WESYP 2019 is now open!

Early Bird Registration: 5th Aug to 18th Aug 2019

Standard Registration: 19th  Aug to 8th Sept

Ticket Types:

●       Full Conference Registration

Includes Gala Dinner and all meals (breakfast, lunch and dinner, tea/coffee breaks) for the duration of congress, and invite to HISTELCON Joint Reception & dinner. Does not include Site Visit.

 

●      Day Tickets

Includes lunch and tea/coffee breaks for the day. Does not include Site Visit, Gala Dinner, breakfast, HISTELCON Joint Reception & Opening Dinner.

●       Gala Dinner (Standard: £45) - add-on only available for Day Delegates.

 

●       Site Visit to wind farm/Demonstration Centre(Standard: £10) - add-on only available for full conference registrants.
