About WESYP

Glasgow, United Kingdom

WESYP 2019 will take place in Glasgow, UK, a city famed for a rich collection of civic beauty, art, culture, music, as well as a long history of innovation. From the television and penicillin to tidal energy turbines, a passion for innovation in Scotland has advanced industry at home and overseas throughout history and Glasgow, as a hub of innovation and budding start-ups, is the best place to experience it all. Glasgow has announced ambitions to be the first zero-carbon city in the United Kingdom – and the natural spot for this year’s WESYP theme of

‘Building a Sustainable Future’
